======================================
 INSTALL YOUR FITNESS APP ON YOUR PHONE
======================================

Your fitness plan is packaged as a PWA (Progressive Web App).
This means it installs like a native app — no App Store needed.
All progress is saved locally on your phone.

─────────────────────────────────────
 OPTION 1 — Easiest: Use a free host
─────────────────────────────────────
Upload the folder to a free static hosting service:

  1. Go to https://netlify.com/drop
  2. Drag the entire "fitness-pwa" folder onto the page
  3. You get a URL like https://random-name.netlify.app
  4. Open that URL on your phone
  5. Follow the install prompt below

─────────────────────────────────────
 OPTION 2 — iPhone/iPad (Safari)
─────────────────────────────────────
  1. Open the URL in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button (rectangle with arrow ⎙) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name it "FitPlan" → tap Add
  5. It appears on your home screen like a native app ✓

─────────────────────────────────────
 OPTION 3 — Android (Chrome)
─────────────────────────────────────
  1. Open the URL in Chrome
  2. A banner appears at the bottom — tap "Install"
     OR tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap Install → it appears on your home screen ✓

─────────────────────────────────────
 WORKS FULLY OFFLINE
─────────────────────────────────────
Once installed, the app works without internet.
(Video links open YouTube when you're online)

 ALL PROGRESS SAVED ON YOUR PHONE
─────────────────────────────────────
Progress is stored in your phone's local storage.
It persists across sessions and app restarts.
It is NOT synced to any server — fully private.
